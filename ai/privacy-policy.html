1. Overview & Commitment

SKIL Lifestyle ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android and Multi-Platform Application collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards user information in strict compliance with the Google Play Developer Policy Framework.

2. Information Collection & Usage

We believe in minimal data collection. The SKIL Lifestyle app processes the following data types:

  • Local Device Preferences: Preferences, theme toggles, and state data stored locally on your device using secure preferences.
  • App Performance Analytics: Diagnostic crash reports and performance metrics collected to maintain 120Hz super-fluid rendering across all Android devices.

3. Permissions & Privacy Minimization

Our app strictly adheres to Google Play permission minimization guidelines:

  • We do NOT request broad storage or contact permissions.
  • We utilize native Android System Pickers for media access.
  • No background location or broad telemetry is gathered.

4. Data Safety & Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. All network traffic is encrypted via standard HTTPS/TLS protocols. Data shared with analytics infrastructure is sanitized and anonymized.

5. Account & Data Deletion Rights

Users have full control over their data. You can clear local app data at any time via your device Settings. To request complete deletion of any synchronized account data, please contact our support team at privacy@skillifestyle.com or visit our Account Deletion portal.
